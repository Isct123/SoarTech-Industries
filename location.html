Location of our factory - Igatpuri

Reasons for picking Igatpuri as our factory location

  • Igatpuri offers close proximity to densly populated and developed cities of Mumbai and Nashik
  • Close proximity to mumbai port
  • Most major supplies, as well as boeing head quaters are in Mumbai
  • Being a smaller town, land is comparativly cheaper
  • Many small hydroelectric dams in the regions to provide water resources
  • Availibitity of skilled, unskilled, and highly skilled labor
